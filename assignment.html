Root Causes of Procrastination:

Four root causes of procrastination are outlined: fear of failure, perfectionism, task overwhelm, and lack of prioritization. Each cause is described in detail, providing insight into why students may struggle with initiating and completing assignments.

Strategies to Overcome Procrastination:

Five practical strategies are offered to help students overcome procrastination:

  1. Breaking down assignments into smaller tasks.
  2. Setting realistic goals and focusing on progress rather than perfection.
  3. Creating a structured study schedule.
  4. Identifying and minimizing distractions.
  5. Seeking support from peers, professors, or academic services.
